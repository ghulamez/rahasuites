Meet Our Executive Director

Crafting memorable dining and sleeping experiences since 2020

At Raha Suites, we believe true hospitality is about more than just a place to stay—it’s about creating comfort, trust, and memorable experiences for every guest who walks through our doors. Located in the heart of Nairobi, Raha Suites was designed to offer a perfect balance of modern elegance, personalized service, and the warmth of Kenyan hospitality. Whether you are visiting for business, leisure, or an extended stay, our commitment is to make you feel at home while delivering service of the highest standard.

Our team is passionate, professional, and dedicated to ensuring that every detail of your stay is handled with care. As Managing Director, I am proud of the culture we have built—one that values excellence, integrity, and genuine connection with our guests. We look forward to welcoming you and being part of your Nairobi experience.
